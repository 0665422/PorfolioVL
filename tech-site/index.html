Smart House

Many companies research and already sell their Smart Home systems. Among them: Amazon Echo, Google Assistant, Nest, Hive, and many others. Most of the Smart Home products don't work with each other, because they are competitors. For example, home temperature system does not communicate with lightning system and etc. By 2030, Smart Home systems are expected to become more efficient, functional, widespread, and affordable.

Future

    Future Smart Home systems will include:
  • Security System
  • Climate and Lightning Control
  • Energy Management
  • Smart Bathroom and Kitchen
  • Automated Home Control (operates Smart Home without human intervention)

Further development may bring Smart City systems that will make our lives even easier.
